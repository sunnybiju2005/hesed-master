CHOIR

Service Team



Our Church Choir is a dedicated group of musicians and singers who lead worship through beautiful music and hymns. We believe that music is a powerful way to praise God and enhance the worship experience. Our choir members are committed to using their musical talents to glorify God and inspire the congregation.

Our Purpose

The Choir serves to enhance worship through music, leading the congregation in praise and worship. We prepare special music for services, feasts, and special occasions. Our goal is to create an atmosphere of worship that draws people closer to God through the beauty of music and song.

Rehearsals & Participation

We hold regular rehearsals to prepare for Sunday services and special events. Choir members work together to learn new songs, harmonize, and perfect our musical offerings. We welcome new members who have a heart for worship and a desire to serve through music, regardless of prior experience.

Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth. Sing to the Lord, praise his name; proclaim his salvation day after day.

Psalm 96:1-2 NIV

Join the Choir

If you have a passion for music and worship, we'd love to have you join our choir! Whether you're an experienced singer or just starting out, there's a place for you. Contact the choir director or church office to learn more about joining our musical ministry.
