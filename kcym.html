KCYM

Service Team



KCYM (Kerala Catholic Youth Movement) is a dynamic youth organization that brings together young people to grow in faith, serve the community, and build meaningful friendships. We provide opportunities for spiritual development, leadership training, and active participation in church and community activities.

Our Vision

KCYM envisions empowered young people who are committed to their faith, actively involved in the church, and making a positive difference in society. We strive to create a vibrant youth community where members can discover their God-given talents and use them to serve others.

Programs & Activities

Our activities include youth retreats, leadership workshops, community service projects, cultural programs, sports events, and regular prayer meetings. We also organize social gatherings and outings to build fellowship and create lasting memories. All activities are designed to strengthen faith and character.

Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity.

1 Timothy 4:12 NIV

Get Involved

If you're a young person looking to grow in faith, make new friends, and serve the community, KCYM is the place for you! We welcome all youth members and encourage active participation. Contact us through the church office or join us at our next meeting.

A beautiful and serene place of worship, St. Paul's Church Thaikkattussey is at the heart of our community. Surrounded by the picturesque landscapes of Thrissur, our church offers a welcoming space for prayer, reflection, and togetherness. It stands as a beacon of faith and harmony, drawing people from near and far to experience its peaceful atmosphere and vibrant spiritual life.
