Mathrusangam

Service Team



Mathrusangam is a fellowship group for mothers and women in our church community. We provide a supportive environment where women can come together for prayer, study, fellowship, and mutual encouragement. Our group focuses on spiritual growth, family life, and serving our church and community.

Our Mission

Mathrusangam aims to strengthen the faith of women and mothers, providing them with opportunities for spiritual growth, prayer, and fellowship. We support one another in our roles as mothers, wives, and women of faith, creating a community where we can share experiences, learn together, and grow in our relationship with God.

Activities & Meetings

We organize regular prayer meetings, Bible study sessions, retreats, and social gatherings. Our activities include discussions on faith, family life, and practical Christian living. We also engage in community service projects and support various church ministries. All mothers and women are welcome to join us.

Join Us

If you're a mother or woman looking for fellowship, spiritual growth, and a supportive community, Mathrusangam welcomes you! We meet regularly and would love to have you join us. Contact the church office or attend one of our meetings to learn more.
