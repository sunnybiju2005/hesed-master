Meet Church Service Team.



St. Paul's Church Thaikkatussery exists to glorify God through vibrant worship, compassionate service, and genuine fellowship among our devoted members, transforming lives in the Thrissur community.

Our Purpose.

At St. Paul's Church Thaikkatussery, our purpose is to cultivate a thriving community of faithful members rooted in Christ, where everyone grows spiritually and serves others with love. We aim to be a beacon of hope in Thrissur, nurturing the hearts of our members through prayer, teaching, and outreach that reflect Christ's compassion. Our purpose unites our members—including our parish priest (Father), church president, treasurer, secretary, and ministry leaders—in building strong families, supporting the needy, and sharing the Gospel with unwavering dedication.

Our Mission.

Our mission at St. Paul's Church Thaikkatussery is to proclaim the Gospel, disciple our committed members, and demonstrate God's love through practical acts of kindness in Thaikkatussery and beyond. We commit to regular worship services, kids programs, and prayer meetings that equip our members for faithful living. By fostering unity and justice among our congregation, led by our Father, president, treasurer, and other office bearers, we seek to impact Thrissur with the transformative power of Christ's message.

St. Paul's Church Thaikkattussery

A beautiful and serene place of worship, St. Paul's Church Thaikkattussey is at the heart of our community. Surrounded by the picturesque landscapes of Thrissur, our church offers a welcoming space for prayer, reflection, and togetherness. It stands as a beacon of faith and harmony, drawing people from near and far to experience its peaceful atmosphere and vibrant spiritual life.
