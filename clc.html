CLC

Service Team



CLC (Catholic Life Community) is a vibrant group dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, community service, and fellowship among our members. We come together to deepen our faith, serve our community, and build lasting relationships centered on Christ.

Our Mission

CLC aims to help members grow in their spiritual journey through prayer, study, and service. We believe in living out our faith in practical ways, making a positive impact in our church and community. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where members can explore and deepen their relationship with God.

Activities & Programs

We organize regular prayer meetings, Bible study sessions, community service projects, and social gatherings. Our activities are designed to strengthen faith, build community, and serve those in need. We welcome all who seek to grow spiritually and serve others with love and compassion.

For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them.

Matthew 18:20 NIV

Join Us

If you're interested in joining CLC or learning more about our activities, please contact us through the church office or attend one of our regular meetings. We welcome new members and look forward to growing together in faith and service.

A beautiful and serene place of worship, St. Paul's Church Thaikkattussey is at the heart of our community. Surrounded by the picturesque landscapes of Thrissur, our church offers a welcoming space for prayer, reflection, and togetherness. It stands as a beacon of faith and harmony, drawing people from near and far to experience its peaceful atmosphere and vibrant spiritual life.
